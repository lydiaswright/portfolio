About

Lydia Wright is a versatile actor with a passion for bringing complex characters to life across film, television, and theatre.

Trained at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA), Lydia has developed a unique ability to inhabit diverse roles, from classical theatre to contemporary screen work. Her performances are characterized by emotional depth, authenticity, and a commitment to storytelling that resonates with audiences.

With experience spanning multiple genres and mediums, Lydia continues to challenge herself with roles that push artistic boundaries and explore the human condition.
