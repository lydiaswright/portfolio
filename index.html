About

Lydia Wright is a young London-based actor, working across stage and screen with a focus on truthful, authentic performances.

She trained in LAMDA Acting with Worlds End Productions before completing a Level 3 Extended Diploma in Performing Arts Practice at Bodens College of Creative Arts in London.

Her stage work spans ensemble and named roles, drawing on a range of skills — from stage combat, physical theatre and Meisner technique to voice acting and improvisation.

Alongside performing, Lydia has experience teaching drama to younger children, improving confidence, imagination and creativity through acting.
